## Dashboard contains

### 3 Pivot Tables
| Analysis | Top Result |
|----------|------------|
| By Category | Electronics ₹23,11,000 |

| By Salesperson | Priya ₹9,12,000 |

### Dashboard Sheet
- Professional title header
- KPI cards — Revenue, Top Category, Top Region, Top Salesperson
- Pie chart — Sales by Category
- Clustered Bar — Sales by Region
- Horizontal Bar — Sales by Salesperson

---

## Key Insights
- Electronics = 84% of total revenue
- South is the strongest region, West the weakest
- Priya is top salesperson
- Each salesperson owns one region — territory quality = performance
